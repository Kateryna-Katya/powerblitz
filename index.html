A Landscape of Still Motion

Something about this valley just... slows everything down.

Lanterns glow without flicker, petals fall without urgency, and paths appear only when the time feels right.

The whole environment's built to slow you down and help you focus. Nothing rushes. Everything responds.

Guardians of Balance

The Seven Spirits — Ebisu, Daikokuten, Bishamonten, Benzaiten, Jurōjin, Hotei, and Fukurokuju — represent different aspects of fortune:
abundance, protection, wisdom, joy, longevity, harmony.

They do not command.

They influence by clearing distractions and shaping the flow of the field.

How the Experience Unfolds

  • The game takes place on a clean, structured field framed by traditional motifs.

  • Elements move smoothly, interactions feel deliberate, and each action subtly reshapes what follows.

  • We made sure everything flows nicely - no jarring interruptions that kill the vibe.

Symbol Dissolution

When certain combinations form, specific symbols quietly fade away.
This reduces visual noise and opens space for clearer patterns.
A Dissolution Bar tracks removed symbols, helping players understand how the field evolves over time.

  • Multiplier Current

    Each successful interaction energizes a segment of the multiplier path.
    After several consecutive activations, the multiplier increases.
    If the flow pauses, the energy slowly dissipates and the multiplier returns to its neutral state.

  • Sustained Dream State

    During special phases, the multiplier continues to grow as long as motion remains uninterrupted.
    When movement ends, the state resolves and the system gently resets.

Luck as a Living System

Luck in this game? Think of it like weather - it builds up, shifts around, and rewards patience.

It builds when you keep going, fades when things get chaotic, and pays off when you find your rhythm.
Focus on what you're doing right now instead of racing to the end.
