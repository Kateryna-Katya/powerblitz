A Landscape of Still Motion

Something about this valley just... slows everything down.

Lanterns glow without flicker, petals fall without urgency, and paths appear only when the time feels right.

The whole environment's built to slow you down and help you focus. Nothing rushes. Everything responds.

Guardians of Balance

The Seven Spirits — Ebisu, Daikokuten, Bishamonten, Benzaiten, Jurōjin, Hotei, and Fukurokuju — represent different aspects of fortune:
abundance, protection, wisdom, joy, longevity, harmony.

They do not command.

They influence by clearing distractions and shaping the flow of the field.
